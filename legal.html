Privacy Policy

ATS ("we", "us") is a single-operator software service at ats.rajasekarselvam.com operated from India. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your control over it.

This policy is compatible with the spirit of India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and references the obligations applicable to a "Data Fiduciary" of small scale.

What we store

  • Account identity: email address, name (if provided), bcrypt-hashed password, account creation + last-login timestamps, IP address of the last login.
  • Broker credentials: your Zerodha (or other) API key + access token, sealed with libsodium so they can be opened only by ATS at runtime with the master key. We never see your broker username or password.
  • AI provider keys: Anthropic / OpenAI / Gemini API keys, sealed identically.
  • Preferences: theme, density, daily AI spend cap, AI mode preference.
  • Operational data: watchlist, paper trade history, P&L attribution, AI call audit log (workflow, provider, model, token counts, cost, your thumb feedback), scanner signal history, append-only audit trail.
  • Telegram chat ID (if you enable Telegram alerts) and a sealed bot token if you provide one.

What we don't store

  • Your broker login password (we use OAuth tokens only).
  • Your AI provider key cleartext outside the sealed vault. (We can read it only inside the server process to call the provider.)
  • The literal text of AI responses beyond the first ~600 characters per response (cached for re-display) and the token counts. The cache is in-memory; everything else lives in your private ai_calls rows.
  • Marketing/behavioural profiles. ATS has no advertising integration.
  • Web tracking pixels, third-party analytics, or session replay tools. (The only client-side JS that loads from a third party is Google Fonts on the landing page.)

Retention

  • Account + sealed credentials: kept until you delete your account (Account → Delete).
  • AI call audit log: trimmed automatically to the last 5,000 calls per user.
  • Append-only audit trail: rotated daily, 7 days kept on disk + archived to Google Drive (rclone). Archive retention is currently indefinite; future "delete archives older than N years" automation is on the roadmap.
  • Backups (nightly Google Drive snapshot): retained for 90 days minimum.

Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Export all your data (Account → Export downloads a JSON of preferences, watchlist, paper trades, AI call history).
  • Delete your account (Account → Danger zone → Delete account). Deletion cascades to all per-user tables and removes your sealed credentials from disk within 1 hour. The append-only audit trail keeps your user_id but no other PII for the SEBI-recommended 7-year retention; if you require complete erasure, email us and we'll honour it.
  • Correct your email or name in Account → Profile.
  • Withdraw consent for Telegram alerts, email digests, or AI processing (set spend cap to 0) without losing access to the rest of the platform.

To exercise any right not visible in the UI, email rajasekarjavaee@gmail.com and we'll respond within 7 business days.

Breach notification

If we become aware of a personal-data breach affecting your account, we'll notify you by email within 72 hours, describing what happened, what data was involved, what we've done, and what you should do (e.g. rotate your broker API key). We'll also notify the Data Protection Board of India per DPDP Act §8(6) when required by law.

Data Processing Addendum (summary)

This short DPA describes how ATS handles personal data on your behalf. It supplements (does not replace) the Privacy Policy above.

Roles

  • You are the Data Principal (subject) for your own personal data.
  • ATS is the Data Fiduciary (controller) for account + operational data we collect from you directly.
  • ATS is a Data Processor for any data you upload (e.g. a CAS PDF you parse via the platform) — we process that data only to deliver the function you requested and don't retain it beyond the session.

Security measures

  • TLS 1.2+ on all public connections (Let's Encrypt cert auto-renewed monthly).
  • libsodium sealed-box encryption at rest for all third-party credentials (broker keys, AI keys, Telegram bot tokens).
  • Master encryption key stored only at /etc/ats/master.key with mode 0440, owner root:ats. Never logged, never backed up to GDrive — local copy only.
  • bcrypt for password hashing.
  • SQLite WAL mode with daily rclone snapshots to Google Drive.
  • Append-only audit log of every authentication + key trade-related action.
  • Optional static IP allowlist + 2FA-confirm-before-trade.

Sub-processors

ATS uses the following third-party services to deliver functionality. Each is invoked only with your explicit configuration (e.g. you saved a Claude API key). We do not share your data with them outside the function they're configured for.

  • Zerodha (Kite Connect) — broker integration, when you connect your account
  • Anthropic / OpenAI / Google (Gemini) — AI provider, only when you save the corresponding API key
  • Google (Google Drive via rclone) — nightly backup storage
  • Let's Encrypt — TLS certificate issuance
  • Telegram — alert delivery, only when you enable it
  • Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (Mumbai region) — VM hosting

International data transfers

The Oracle Cloud VM hosting ATS is located in India (Mumbai). AI provider APIs (Anthropic / OpenAI / Gemini) are reached over the public internet and may process data in the United States or EU. By saving an AI provider key, you authorise that limited transfer for the purpose of generating responses.

Term & termination

This DPA is in force for as long as you have an active ATS account. On termination, ATS deletes your personal data per the Retention section, except where extended retention is required for SEBI / income-tax audit purposes (audit trail entries retained for 7 years).
